Blueberry Baked Oats

  • 17 reviews

Swap your regular porridge for a healthy baked version, packed with oats, juicy blueberries and crunchy almonds. It's a warming breakfast for cold mornings

  • Servings: 6
  • Prep Time: 5 minutes
  • Cook Time: 35 minutes
  • Total Time: 40 minutes

Nutrition Facts (Per Serving)

    Kcal 271 Fat 11g Saturates 1g Carbs 34g Sugar 10g Fibre 6g Portein 10g Salt 0.35g

The Ingredients

  • 500ml almond milk
  • 200g jumbo porridge oats
  • 2 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 small ripe banana, mashed
  • Â½ tsp almond extract or 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 450g blueberries, plus extra to serve
  • 30g whole, skin-on almonds, roughly chopped
  • milk or fat-free yogurt and honey, to serve (optional)

The Process

  1. Heat the oven to 200C/180C fan/gas 6.
  2. Mix all of the ingredients together in a large bowl.
  3. Tip the mixture into a 2-litre ovenproof dish, then bake for 30-35 mins until piping hot in the middle.
  4. Serve warm with a little milk or yogurt, honey and extra blueberries, if you like.
